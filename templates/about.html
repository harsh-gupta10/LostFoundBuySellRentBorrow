About IIIT-H Lost and Found

Welcome to IIIT-H Lost and Found, a platform designed to connect the IIIT-H community and facilitate the process of finding, buying, or borrowing items within the campus.

Motive

At IIIT-H Lost and Found, our mission is to provide a convenient and secure platform for members of the IIIT-H community to post, discover, and acquire lost and found items. We aim to foster a sense of community by enabling individuals to connect with each other and facilitate the exchange of items.

How it Works

  1. Post: You can quickly establish a listing by including information about the item if you've lost anything or found something that belongs to someone else. This makes it possible for other people to locate and recover their misplaced property..
  2. Browse and Search: Look through the product listings that have been posted on our platform. To find things that meet your requirements or interests, use the search box or peruse the categories..
  3. Connect and Transact: Through our secure messaging platform, you can get in touch with the owner immediately after discovering an item of interest. Organise a meeting with them and work together to finish the deal..

Privacy and Security

We recognise the value of security and privacy. Only the essential information required for communication and transaction reasons will be shared between users, and your personal information will be kept private.
